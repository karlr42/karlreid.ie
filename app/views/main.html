Welcome

This is the personal site of Karl Reid, a software developer based in Dublin, Ireland.

Currently I work at General Audit Tool, a powerful auditing platform for cloud services.

I work mainly on our GAT Shield product, an auditing solution for G Suite administrators to monitor and enforce policies for a deployment of Chrome devices.

As a full stack developer, I work on developing and maintaining features across all levels of the product, from the client browser extension, to the server backend and the administrator dashboard.

In the past I worked on CipherApps, a startup that developed a security solution for cloud services.

The company was spun out from the School of Computer Science and Statistics at Trinity College Dublin. I was a co founder and the lead developer.

CipherApps raised funding from Enterprise Ireland and was accepted into the Wayra accelerator.
